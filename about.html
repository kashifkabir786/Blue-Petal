Meet Resham Marwa

Founder & Creative Director

From Defence Roots to Design Excellence: A Journey of Creativity

As the daughter of a retired Indian Air Force Pilot, my life has been shaped by discipline, adaptability, and a deep appreciation for creativity. My journey in design began as a childhood passion for art, craft, and sketching, eventually leading me to establish Blue Petals Design Studio.

Professional Evolution

Resham's Boutique (2008-2015)

Started my journey with Indian wear design and stitching, hosting successful exhibitions including two organized by Tata Steel at Lotus Hall and Aashiana Jamshedpur.

Blue Petals Design Studio (2016-Present)

Specializing in contemporary Western wear, creating comfortable and stylish designs for women aged 13-85. Our pieces have been featured in Karigar (A Tata Steel shop) and continue to evolve with modern fashion trends.

Our Brands

Blue Petals Design Studio

At Blue Petals Design Studio, we believe in the timeless appeal of cotton and the versatility of western wear. Our designs focus on comfortable, stylish cuts that cater to the modern woman's needs across all seasons. We're committed to creating fashion that's both contemporary and accessible, embracing the global trend toward sophisticated western wear.

My Indians

My Indians celebrates the rich heritage of Indian fashion with a modern twist. We specialize in creating elegant Indian wear that combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs. Our collection features carefully curated fabrics and intricate details that honor India's textile legacy while meeting the style preferences of today's fashion-conscious individuals.
