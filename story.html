The Journey of Blue Petals Design Studio

By Resham Marwa

Early Life & Education

Coming from a Defence Background with my father being a retired Indian Air Force Pilot, my journey in design began early. After completing my BA (Hons) from St. Xavier's Calcutta, I dedicated 9 years to pursuing fashion design education across prestigious institutions:

  • • IIFT New Delhi
  • • MIT Melbourne, Australia
  • • Tagore Society Jamshedpur
  • • DBMS Career Academy Jamshedpur

I further enhanced my expertise with a PGDHRM from Symbiosis Center for Distant Learning, Pune.

Professional Journey

My professional journey began in 2008 with 'Resham's Boutique', where I spent 7 years specializing in Indian Wear design and stitching. During this time, I:

  • • Hosted 3 solo exhibitions at Lotus Hall, Aashiana Jamshedpur
  • • Participated in 4 exhibitions, including 2 organized by Tata Steel

Birth of Blue Petals Design Studio

In 2016, I founded Blue Petals Design Studio, focusing on Female Western tops and Cocktail Dresses. Over the past 6 years, we've:

  • • Specialized in Western Dresses and Tops on Calico
  • • Created designs for Karigar (A Tata Steel shop)
  • • Catered to women aged 13-85 years
  • • Focused on cotton-based comfortable western wear with stylish cuts
